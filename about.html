How We Started

The Kip Story

Kip Smart Living Enterprise started from a simple frustration — spending hours scrolling Amazon reviews trying to figure out which kitchen gadgets were actually worth buying.

We built Kip to be the resource we wished existed: a curated, honest, easy-to-browse destination for discovering the best kitchen and smart home products — without the noise.

Today we help tens of thousands of shoppers every month find gadgets that genuinely improve their daily lives.
