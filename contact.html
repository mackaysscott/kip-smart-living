Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if a product is still available on Amazon? ▼
Click any "View on Amazon" button — it will take you directly to the live Amazon listing where you can see the current price, availability, and stock status in real time.
Do you accept product submissions or review requests? ▼
Yes! If you have a product you'd like us to feature or review, email us at [email protected] with the subject line "Product Review Request" and we'll take a look.
Do you offer sponsored posts or paid placements? ▼
We do work with select brands on sponsored content, but we always clearly disclose paid partnerships and only feature products we genuinely believe are worth recommending to our audience.
Can I suggest a product for you to review? ▼
Absolutely! We love reader suggestions. Use the contact form above or email us directly at [email protected] — many of our best reviews come from reader recommendations.
How does the Amazon affiliate program work? ▼
When you click our Amazon links and make a purchase, we earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. The price you pay on Amazon is exactly the same. This commission helps us maintain the site and continue producing free content.
I found a broken link or incorrect price — how do I report it? ▼
Thank you for helping us improve! Use the contact form above and select "Website Issue / Bug Report" — we'll fix it as quickly as possible and we genuinely appreciate the heads up.
