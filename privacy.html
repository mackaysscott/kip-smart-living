🔒 Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This page explains exactly how we handle your data.

Last Updated: March 1, 2025  |  Effective Date: March 1, 2025

📋 Quick Summary: We don't sell your data. We use basic analytics to improve our content. We earn affiliate commissions from Amazon at no extra cost to you. That's the simple version — the full details are below.

1. Who We Are

Kip Smart Living Enterprise ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at mackaysscott.github.io/kip-smart-living. You can contact us at [email protected].

2. Amazon Affiliate Disclosure

Kip Smart Living Enterprise is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com.

When you click a product link on our site and make a qualifying purchase on Amazon, we may earn a small commission. This comes at absolutely no extra cost to you. The price you pay on Amazon is identical whether or not you use our links.

As required by the FTC, all affiliate relationships are clearly disclosed on every page of this website.

3. Information We Collect

We collect minimal personal information. Here is what may be collected:

  • Contact form submissions: If you use our contact form, your name, email address, and message are sent directly to our Gmail inbox via your email client. We do not store this data on any server.
  • Newsletter subscriptions: If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address to send you deal alerts and gadget picks.
  • Analytics data: We may use Google Analytics or similar tools to collect anonymised data about how visitors use the site — such as which pages are visited, time spent, and device type. This data contains no personally identifiable information.
  • Cookies: See Section 5 below for full cookie details.

We do not collect payment information, passwords, or sensitive personal data of any kind.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to messages and enquiries sent via the contact form
  • To send newsletter emails to subscribers who opted in (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • To analyse site traffic and improve our content and user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

5. Cookies

Our website may use cookies — small text files stored on your device — for the following purposes:

  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with the site (via Google Analytics)
  • Amazon cookies: When you click Amazon affiliate links, Amazon may place cookies on your device as part of their affiliate tracking system. This is governed by Amazon's Privacy Policy, not ours.
  • Preference cookies: We may store lightweight preferences (such as wishlist items) in your browser's localStorage.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Note that some site functionality may be affected.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to Amazon and other third-party websites. Once you leave our site, we are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policy of any site you visit.

7. Data Security

Since we are a static website hosted on GitHub Pages, we do not operate servers that store personal data. Contact form messages are delivered directly to our email client without being stored on any intermediate server.

We take reasonable precautions to protect any data we do handle, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately at [email protected].

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at [email protected].

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: [email protected]
  • Contact form: kipsmartliving.github.io/contact.html
